Our Premium Zip Hoodie is the perfect blend of comfort and style for your everyday urban adventures. Crafted from a heavyweight cotton blend fabric, this hoodie offers exceptional warmth and durability while maintaining a soft feel against your skin.

The modern fit is designed to be flattering without being restrictive, giving you the freedom to move while maintaining a sleek silhouette. The split kangaroo pocket provides ample storage for your essentials, with a hidden inner compartment to securely store your phone or wallet.

Each hoodie features our signature minimalist embroidery on the left chest and a subtle woven label at the hem. The premium YKK zipper ensures smooth operation and long-lasting durability, while the metal pull adds a touch of sophistication.

Whether you're layering up for a chilly morning commute or winding down after a workout, this versatile hoodie is the perfect companion for the modern urban lifestyle.
