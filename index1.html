About Our Brand

Crafted with Passion

At Urban Threads, we believe that clothing is more than just fabric—it's a form of self-expression. Each piece in our collection is thoughtfully designed and crafted with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring both style and durability.

Our team of designers draws inspiration from urban landscapes, contemporary art, and street culture to create pieces that are both timeless and on-trend. We're committed to using premium materials and ethical manufacturing processes to deliver products that look good and feel even better.

Sustainable Practices

Sustainability isn't just a buzzword for us—it's a core value that guides everything we do. From sourcing eco-friendly materials to implementing energy-efficient manufacturing processes, we're constantly looking for ways to reduce our environmental footprint.

We've partnered with local artisans and ethical factories to ensure fair wages and safe working conditions throughout our supply chain. By choosing Urban Threads, you're not just investing in quality clothing—you're supporting a more sustainable and equitable fashion industry.

Premium Materials

We source only the highest quality fabrics and materials to ensure comfort, durability, and style that lasts.

Thoughtful Design

Every stitch, seam, and detail is carefully considered to create pieces that are both functional and fashionable.

Ethical Production

We partner with factories that prioritize worker welfare and environmental responsibility in their operations.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find my size?

We provide detailed size guides for each product category. You can find the size chart on each product page under the "Size Guide" link. Our products generally follow standard sizing, but we recommend measuring yourself and comparing with our size chart for the best fit. If you're between sizes, we typically recommend sizing up for a more comfortable fit.

What is your shipping policy?

We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $75 within the continental United States. Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping is available for an additional fee and delivers within 1-2 business days. International shipping is available to select countries, with delivery times varying by location. All orders are processed within 1-2 business days.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and with all original tags attached. Return shipping is free for exchanges, but customers are responsible for return shipping costs for refunds unless the item is defective or we made an error. Please visit our Returns page to initiate the return process.

How do I care for my Urban Threads products?

Each product comes with specific care instructions on the tag. Generally, we recommend machine washing cold with like colors, using mild detergent, and avoiding bleach. Tumble dry on low heat or lay flat to dry for best results. For premium items like our hoodies and jackets, turning them inside out before washing helps preserve the colors and prints. Always refer to the care label for specific instructions.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes, we offer digital gift cards in denominations ranging from $25 to $200. Gift cards are delivered via email and can be used for online purchases. They never expire and can be used across multiple purchases until the balance is depleted. Gift cards make perfect presents for friends and family who appreciate quality streetwear.

Are your products sustainable?

Sustainability is a core value at Urban Threads. We're continuously working to improve our environmental footprint by using organic and recycled materials where possible, reducing waste in our production processes, and partnering with factories that adhere to strict environmental standards. Our Eco Collection features products made from 100% organic cotton or recycled materials. We're transparent about our journey toward sustainability and share regular updates on our progress.
